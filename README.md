# 🛒 Superstore Analytics Dashboard (Power BI)

## 1️⃣ Introduction

This project is a **Superstore Sales Analytics Dashboard** built using **Power BI**.

The dashboard analyzes sales data to understand:

* Sales performance
* Profit performance
* Return rate
* Product performance
* State-wise profit
* Customer segment contribution
* Year-over-Year comparison

It helps business users quickly understand how the company is performing and where improvements are needed.

---

## 2️⃣ Business Problem

The Superstore company sells products across different categories, states, and customer segments.

But the company is facing problems like:

* ❓ Which products are making losses?
* ❓ Which states are highly profitable?
* ❓ Is sales growing compared to last year?
* ❓ What is the return order percentage?
* ❓ Which customer segment generates the most revenue?

Management needs a **clear and interactive dashboard** to make data-driven decisions.

---

## 3️⃣ Goal of the Dashboard

The main goals of this dashboard are:

1. Track overall Sales, Profit, and Return %
2.  Compare current performance vs previous year
3. Identify profitable and loss-making products
4.  Analyze state-wise profit
5.  Understand customer segment contribution
6. Support better business decisions

---

## 4️⃣ Walkthrough of Key Visuals

### 🔹 1. KPI Cards (Top Section)

* **Sales:** $2.33M
* **Profit:** $292.30K
* **% Returned Orders:** 5.79%

Each KPI also shows:

* Previous Year value (PY)
* Growth vs Previous Year (%)

👉 This gives a quick performance summary.

---

### 🔹 2. Sales vs Previous Year (Line Chart)

* Shows monthly sales trend
* Compares current year vs previous year

Helps answer:

* Are we growing?
* Which months performed better?
* Are there seasonal patterns?

---

### 🔹 3. Profit by Product (Bar Chart)

* Shows profit for each product category
* Uses color formatting:

  * 🟢 Green → High profit
  * 🔴 Red → Loss

Helps identify:

* Loss-making products
* High-performing products

Example:

* Tables are generating losses.
* Copiers and Phones are highly profitable.

---

### 🔹 4. Profit by State (Map)

* Shows profit distribution across U.S. states
* Darker color = Higher profit

Helps identify:

* High-performing regions
* Weak states needing attention

---

### 🔹 5. Sales by Segment (Donut Chart)

Customer Segments:

* Consumer – 50.32%
* Corporate – 30.77%
* Home Office – 18.92%

Helps understand:

* Which segment drives most revenue
* Where to focus marketing efforts

---

## 5️⃣ Key Insights

📌 Sales increased by **47.16% vs Previous Year**
📌 Profit increased by **48.85% vs Previous Year**
📌 Return rate improved (decreased by 2.95%)

📌 Technology products generate the highest profit
📌 Tables category is loss-making
📌 Consumer segment contributes more than 50% of sales
📌 Some states generate significantly higher profit than others

---

## 6️⃣ Business Impact

This dashboard helps the company:

✅ Focus on high-profit products
✅ Improve or discontinue loss-making items
✅ Target high-performing customer segments
✅ Optimize regional sales strategy
✅ Monitor return rates and improve customer satisfaction
✅ Make faster and data-driven decisions

Overall, this dashboard transforms raw sales data into **actionable business insights**.
